This game is a fun experiment to explore which toilet sign (bathroom icon) you prefer. The aim is to gather anonymous data on gender-based preferences and any potential side biases. Your participation helps us better understand these trends in a lighthearted way.

I am sorry to exclude NBs, but this game requires a presumption of a gender binary, otherwise it does not really work :( sorry.
